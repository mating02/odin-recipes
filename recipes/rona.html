Introducing: The Rona Special

Description

The Rona Special is for all people who want to experience a delicate insight into awe spreading, pandemic causing and pathogenic foods. The Rona Special is simple to make but incredibly unique because somehow people don't try this out anymore. Become one of the few who are eager to try.

Ingredients

  • bat
  • (fresh!) herbs

Instruction Manual

  1. go into the bat cave
  2. catch a bat using your right hand(this asserts dominance so that it will be easier to catch bats in the future)
