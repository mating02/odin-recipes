Introducing: British Beans and Toast

Description

This is truly the British Dream. This dish requires a high level of delicacy of feeling in order to get just the right amount

of the despicably looking but succulent beans onto the plain toast (as one can see in the picture)

Ingredients

  • 2 bean cans
  • the whitest and plainest toasts you can find
  • expired sausages

Instruction Manual (Read carefully!)

  1. poke a hole in the bean cans
  2. carefully let the bean soup pour onto the toasts
  3. put the sausages on top
  4. Enjoy Englands most luscious and flavoursome breakfast!
