Our House Special: The Nasty Patty

Description

The Nasty Patty is a very tasty and satiating dish. It is easy to make but the preparation for this dish takes a lot of time and patience. Once finished, one can observe the sheer beauty of this burger.

Ingredient

  • 2 alaskan burger buns
  • The infamous Ohio State burger patty
  • salad
  • olive marmalade

Instruction Manual

  1. take them buns and throw them into your garden
  2. do the same thing with the patty
  3. do the same thing with the salad
  4. NOW, do the same thing with the olive marmalade

    (Note that it is important to do the things in that Order!)

  5. wait
  6. put the parts together
Voila!
